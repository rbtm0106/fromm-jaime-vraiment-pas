How to Convert .TS to .MP4

To keep downloads blazing fast and prevent your browser from crashing, videos are saved in their raw .ts (Transport Stream) format.

Thanks gemini

Instructions:

  1. Download the Converter Tool
  2. Unzip the downloaded ts_to_mp4.zip file.
  3. Drag and drop your downloaded .ts video directly onto the .bat file.
  4. Done! A pristine MP4 will instantly appear next to it.
